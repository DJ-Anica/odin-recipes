Pizza Bagels

With pizza bagels, you can eat THREE "whole pizzas" without the guilt of actually eating three whole pizzas.

Ingredients

  • 4 full-sized bagels of your choice, halved
  • 1/2 cup store-bought or homemade pizza sauce
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • For toppings, 6 ounces of whatever the f@#% you want, including pineapple

Steps

  1. pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Liberally apply pizza sauce on each halved bagel (the sliced side, not the outside part).
  3. Liberally, and I mean liberally top with cheese.
  4. Add as many toppings as you like.
  5. Top it with more cheese, for good luck.
  6. Bake in oven for 8 minutes, or until the cheese looks sexy.
  7. Eat all your pizza bagels in one sitting.
