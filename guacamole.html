Guacamole

El Jefe of dips.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe haas avocados, peeled, pitted
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 serrano chile, seeded/deveined & finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin

Steps

  1. In a medium mixing bowl, add the avocados & lime juice.
  2. Add the salt, cumin, and cayenne and lightly mash.
  3. Fold in the tomato, onion, cilantro, chile, and lime juice and mix to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or mash
